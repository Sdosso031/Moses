About Us

Health Care is one of the top hospital in Liberia with its professional doctors.
It was established in 2015 by the European Nations in Liberia to help the country with its lack of medical facilities. Since its establisment, Health Care won major honors in and out of Liberia for its best practice. At Health Care we have surgeons, dentist, osteologists, etc. We also have a modern laboratory for tests. Our medicines are directly from Europe and are well tested. In Liberia we received many accolades from the Minister of Health, Dr. Willehmina Jallah for helping the country during the pandemic and Ebola crisis.

24/7 Service

At Health Care we are available everyday and everytime of the day.
Our services is here to help people around Liberia to get free and fast treatments across the whole country. We have very fast ambulances, pharmacies and clinics across all the country.
